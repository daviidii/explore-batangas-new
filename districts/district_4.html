San Juan City, Batangas

Experience the unspoiled beauty of San Juan, Batangas - a hidden gem that's waiting to be discovered. From its pristine beaches to its vibrant culture, San Juan has everything you need for a memorable vacation.

San Juan's breathtaking coastline is one of its key attractions. The area is home to a number of stunning beaches, including Laiya, which offers pristine waters and snow-white sand. Enjoy a relaxing swim or some sunbathing while taking in the peaceful beach atmosphere. You can also try snorkeling or diving and explore the colorful marine life of San Juan's coral reefs.

And or those who love adventure and nature, San Juan has a lot to offer too. Take a trip down to its known mangrove forset, a majestic river that offers breathtaking views of its mangrove formations.

San Juan won't let you down when it comes to eating, either. Enjoy a feast of local specialties like adobong pusit and sinigang na ulo ng salmon as well as fresh fish. Additionally, you can try the town's renowned panutsa, a salty and sweet peanut brittle that's great for snacking. San Juan, Batangas, is the ideal location for a special holiday because of its stunning natural surroundings, dynamic culture, and mouthwatering cuisine.

Naambon Falls

Twas tradithe history of Naambon Falls dates back to ancient times, as the area ionally inhabited by the Tagalog people, who considered the waterfall a sacred site. In more recent history, Naambon Falls was rediscovered by local residents in the mid-20th century. In the years that followed, the waterfall became a popular destination for locals looking to escape the heat and enjoy the natural beauty of the area.

As tourism in San Juan developed in the late 20th and early 21st centuries, Naambon Falls began to attract more visitors from outside the immediate area. Today, the waterfall is a well-known attraction in San Juan, drawing visitors from across the Philippines and beyond.

Mangrove Cruise

In San Juan, Batangas, mangrove cruises are a relatively recent growth in the local tourism sector. Local government officials and environmental organizations started to appreciate San Juan's huge mangrove forests as an ecological resource and prospective tourist destination in the early 2000s.

Virgin Beach

The resort's beginnings can be traced to the early 1980s, when a group of friends from Manila decided to construct a vacation home after learning about the beach's seclusion. A businessman, a restaurateur, and a fashion designer were among the participants. The group expanded the property over time and began renting out the vacation property to family and friends.

To accommodate the escalating demand, the resort underwent a significant refurbishment and extension towards the beginning of the twenty-first century. Virgin Beach Resort is currently well-known for its immaculate beach, clean waters, and laid-back environment and has gained popularity among both domestic and foreign guests.
